How it works

📝

Report in plain language

No forms, no checkboxes. "Cleaned the whole kitchen" is a valid input. The AI rates difficulty 1–10 and picks which stat it trains: Body, Mind, Home or Wellbeing.

🐣

Your pet evolves

Stats hatch the egg and drive evolution — Baby, Adult, Master — branching by your dominant stat. Gold is separate and purely cosmetic: furniture for its isometric room.

💀

The Phoenix rule

Health decays if you disappear. At zero the pet crystallizes — dead. The only revival: come back with a task the judge rates difficulty ≥ 9. Your comeback has to be real.

📸

Photo-verified focus

Pomodoro sessions ask for one photo halfway through; Gemini Vision checks you're actually doing what you claimed. Verified sessions multiply the reward.

👨‍👩‍👧

Shared pets

Groups of 2–6 share one pet that lives on collective effort. A live availability semaphore shows who's working, and when several members work at once, Frenzy mode boosts everyone.

🛡️

Hard to cheat

Everything resolves server-side: rewards, shop, evolution. The judge scores plausibility and pays liars less. Duplicate tasks and grinding pay less too.

Get it

Google Play release is in progress. Meanwhile the whole project is open source — server, client and the shop catalog.
